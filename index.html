Welcome to Asexuality India

Asexuality is little or no sexual attraction to anyone, or low or absent interest in sexual activity. It is a wide spectrum and every Ace is unique and different. So if you identify as one or are still figuring things out, welcome home! We hope to bring to you as much information and support as we can. Keep yourself updated with the Asexual scenes in India!
