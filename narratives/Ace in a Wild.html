Ace in a Wild Space: Being Hetero-Romantic Asexual

POSTED ON OCTOBER 27, 2016 BY POORNIMA

“My foremost memory around relationships was as a child when I decided I must not get married. I saw no reason or need for this. This went on into the formative growing years and into college. Relationships? Yes! These were romantic in nature which grew intense as I got to know my partner intellectually as well as emotionally. Now, when it came to sex, I realized this was not something I wanted, desired or needed.”
